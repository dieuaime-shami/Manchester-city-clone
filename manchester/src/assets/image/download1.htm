Introduction

Imagine it's 2035 and our University has changed the world for the better. How did we do it? What role did you play?

Manchester 2035 is our bold new strategy – and you can help shape it.

Help us identify and accelerate our strategic ambitions for the next decade.

  • Make the leap

What does a truly great university look like in the 21st century?

Manchester 2035 is about all of us – students, staff, alumni and partners – coming together to shape our strategy and take on the challenges of the future.

Through collaboration, we'll consider who we want to be and how we get there, creating a joint plan that reflects our aspirations and benefits the communities we serve.

Your voice is crucial in reimagining what's possible.

Watch our Ideas Lab video to see just some of the engagement so far:

Where are we up to?

Phase one: Listening and discussing

October 2024–January 2025

You shared your ideas online and in-person, and we discussed them at staff, student and community and civic workshops.

Phase two: Testing proposals

February–May 2025

We created bold proposals based on your ideas and tested these with our community.

Phase three: Approval and publish

June–October 2025

With your input, we'll shape and develop the final strategy, outlining the actions you can take to help us achieve it.

Foundations and leaps

Our wide-ranging conversations in phase one ran across five key themes:

  • Reimagining student experiences
  • Becoming one university
  • Accelerating research impact
  • Advancing our social responsibility
  • Funding a world-class university of the future

These discussions confirmed our foundations and pointed to bold, cross-cutting ambitions for 2035.

Sprint groups formed of our University Executive, leadership groups and Students' Union Executive then turned your ideas into our 'foundations' and 'leaps’.

Foundations

Foundations are central to our identity and underpin the university we need to be in the future:

Social responsibility is embedded across everything we do, shaping our curriculum and inspiring students to become graduates who drive positive change for society and the environment.

At home in Manchester, but global in everything we do, we connect students to skills, our community to ideas, and our research to solutions that drive local growth and global change.

With a unified vision and well-designed, integrated systems, we empower our people to do their best work and deliver meaningful impact as one University.

Driven by a commitment to discovery and truth-seeking, our teaching and research inspire innovation and create knowledge and graduates who shape the future.

Equity, diversity, and inclusion are at the heart of our community. We nurture and respect individual differences, while coming together as one University to ensure every voice is valued and every individual feels connected, seen, and heard.

Leaps

Leaps are the bold, future-focused ideas that will set our university apart by 2035. Building on our foundations, they push boundaries, foster innovation and ensure we thrive in a rapidly changing world.
 
Learn more about each leap in our Manchester 2035 podcast. We explored the big ideas for each in our Ideas Lab.

We're currently thinking hard about how our leaps best reflect the views, ideas and concerns we've heard throughout this process, from the whole university community.

Project timeline

The Manchester 2035 consultation process was formally launched by President and Vice-Chancellor, Professor Duncan Ivison, at the University’s Foundation Day on Wednesday, 2 October 2024.

From October through to December, online and in-person consultation workshops took place with staff and students. An online feedback tool was also made available to capture staff, student, alumni and partner views.

Consultation feedback was collected and analysed, and is being drafted into proposals of what could be included in the new strategic plan.

In February our University Executive, in collaboration with leadership teams and the Students’ Union, formed sprint groups to transform your insights into bold, cross-cutting proposals – our leaps.

In April we launched our Manchester 2035 Ideas Lab – physical and online spaces to invite further feedback and engage our whole University community to refine and prioritise these ideas.

We’ll move into the drafting phase and gain formal approval of our strategy.
  
An early preview of the direction of travel will be shared with colleagues at the September staff townhall, ahead of the final strategy launch in October. The implementation phase post-October will be purposeful, structured and inclusive – and we’ll continue to create opportunities for you to be involved.
